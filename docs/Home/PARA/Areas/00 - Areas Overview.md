# 🗺️ Areas Overview

> Ongoing responsibilities that don't have an end date. You maintain these indefinitely.

---

| Area | What it covers |
|---|---|
| **Homelab** | Your self-hosted infrastructure — keep it running, secure, documented |
| **Home Automation** | Smart home setup — maintenance, new integrations |
| **Music Production** | Maschine, gear, practice |
| **Personal Knowledge** | This vault — weekly reviews, inbox processing |
| **Career** | Skills, learning, professional development |
| **Health & Lifestyle** | Exercise, food, Friesland walks |

## Notes

- Areas feed into Projects: if your homelab area surfaces a problem to fix, that becomes a project
- Each Area has a corresponding **Resource note** with collected references
- Review your areas weekly — are you maintaining the standard you want?
